History of Bill's

The Owner...

Billy Frank Latham was born in 1940 right here in Chester County.

He has lived in Chester County for most of his life, except for living out of state for one year.

He has been a proud owner of Bill's Bar B Q since 2000 and the building has been a barbeque establishment since 1983.

Billy Hopes to give the bussiness to his grandson once he is ready for retirement.

A little about the business...

The pig's used for the meat are from the a slaughter house in lexington called "Hays Slaughterhouse".

The pigs can take anywhere from 18 to 22 hours depending on the size of the hog!

The pigs can weigh anywhere from 250-325 pounds however Bill only gets to use 75-80 pounds of meat.
